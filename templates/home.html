Welcome to the Adaptive LED Matrix

This application allows you to upload a video for processing. You can detect lanes, potholes, and people in the video.

To get started, navigate to the Upload Page to upload your video.

Key Features

Lane Detection

Detect lane markings in videos to assist in driving tasks and road safety.

Pothole Detection

Identify potholes to enhance road maintenance and user safety.

Person Detection

Detect people in videos for applications in surveillance and safety.
